## Publication-Level Notes

### NKS-PUB-000002 — Media Blitz Is the Publishing Subsystem

The draft has a clear distinction between knowledge manufacturing and public distribution. Its strongest line is the separation of corpus supply chain, publishing subsystem, and control plane. No substantive rewrite is required.

### NKS-PUB-000003 — GitHub Is the Control Plane for This Knowledge System

The workspace/control-plane distinction is clear and practically demonstrated by the repository. Public wording should continue to describe GitHub as the current control plane rather than a permanent or universally preferred architecture.

### NKS-PUB-000004 — The No Idle State Rule

The draft successfully distinguishes continuous execution from reckless autonomy by naming valid stop conditions. Those conditions are essential and must remain prominent.

### NKS-PUB-000005 — Clarity Is an Economic Instrument

The argument is strong when bounded to avoidable organizational friction. Meta-analytic evidence supports an association between role ambiguity and lower performance, but not a specific ROI or universal causal claim. A source section must be included.

### NKS-PUB-000006 — Coherence Beats Compliance

The draft appropriately argues that process can support coherence but cannot substitute for sensemaking. No substantive revision is required.

### NKS-PUB-000007 — How Executives Turn Ambiguity into Capability

The conversion sequence is clear and reusable. The draft should retain its distinction between forcing certainty and creating enough structure for intelligent action.

### NKS-PUB-000008 — The Resume Should Not Be the First Signal

The draft avoids simple self-promotion by framing public work as evidence of operating philosophy. It must not imply that public proof guarantees opportunity or replaces formal assessment.

### NKS-PUB-000009 — Agentic AI Requires Human Oversight Architecture

NIST supports the governance architecture described: oversight roles, monitoring, incident response, escalation, override, fallback, recovery, and deactivation. The draft is editorially sound once citations are inserted.

### NKS-PUB-000010 — Security Automation Needs Guardrails

The distinction between capability and operating envelope is clear. The draft is editorially sound once current NIST citations are inserted.

### NKS-PUB-000011 — Agentic DevOps Beyond CI/CD

The draft correctly reframes delivery around evidence and risk rather than speed alone. SSDF and SLSA provide current primary-source support for secure lifecycle, provenance, and verification. The draft is editorially sound once citations are inserted.

### NKS-PUB-000012 — Self-Healing Systems Need Governance

The draft accurately treats remediation as an authority model. Kubernetes provides a current concrete example of bounded self-healing and documented limitations. The draft is editorially sound once citations are inserted.
