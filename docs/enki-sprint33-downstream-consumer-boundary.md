# Enki Sprint 33 Downstream Consumer Boundary

> Non-authoritative implementation note. Canonical sprint and work-item records govern completion state.

Sprint 33 integrates Media Blitz, Career Intelligence and Placement, and Personal Cognitive Continuity as downstream Enki consumers through stable TEST-only product contracts.

The integration consumes governed retrieval projections. It does not receive a canonical repository, canonical writer, or direct approval-consumption primitive.

## Product boundaries

- Media Blitz receives governed source projections and may construct publication-shaped TEST packages, no-effect delivery receipts, synthetic feedback, and calibration artifacts.
- Career Intelligence and Placement receives governed professional-state projections. Application-preparation paths require exact external-action authorization bound to the product boundary and projection hash; TEST execution still has no external effect.
- Personal Cognitive Continuity receives governed historical/current projections only when explicit human authority, consent, privacy acknowledgement, and temporal-authority acknowledgement all permit use. Correction and retraction are emitted as downstream governed requests, never direct canonical mutations.

## Package identity

Downstream package identity binds the product suite, consumer intent, and exact projection hash. Different intents over the same governed projection therefore remain independently receipted and cannot collide in the no-effect delivery adapter.

## Invariants

- TEST-only execution.
- No production effects.
- No direct canonical writes.
- No cross-product tenant, subject, purpose, audience, or authority reuse.
- Product outputs remain noncanonical until a separate governed Enki operation authorizes any canonical mutation.
