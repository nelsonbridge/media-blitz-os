# Executive Evidence Bank

## Purpose

Convert executive-value source material into reusable, proof-bounded evidence objects for briefings, interviews, resumes, opportunity matching, and future publications.

This bank is not a resume. It is a governed evidence layer.

## Claim Disposition Legend

- **Citable** — source-backed and safe for external use.
- **Internal Evidence** — usable for private positioning; requires source review before public use.
- **Personal Claim** — usable when clearly attributed to personal experience.
- **Illustrative Framing** — useful as model/metaphor, not proof.
- **Hold** — insufficient support.
- **Remove** — likely misleading or too risky.
