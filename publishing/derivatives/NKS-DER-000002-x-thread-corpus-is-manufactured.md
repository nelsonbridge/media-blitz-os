## Thread Draft

### 1

A corpus is not a folder.

It is a manufacturing output.

### 2

Documents, chats, notes, and whitepapers are source material.

They may contain durable knowledge, but they are not automatically reusable.

### 3

A corpus begins when ideas are extracted, classified, assigned lineage, connected to other ideas, and made available for downstream use.

### 4

Most knowledge systems fail because they confuse accumulation with architecture.

They preserve information without operationalizing it.

### 5

The useful sequence is:

Source material → extraction → artifact → corpus → knowledge graph → publication / decision / strategy.

### 6

The archive is not the asset.

The asset is the process that turns raw material into reusable knowledge.

### 7

Do not wait to find the corpus.

Build it.
