## Core Post Draft

Compliance can look like alignment while coherence is still missing.

A process can be followed.
A meeting can happen.
A dashboard can turn green.
A checklist can be complete.

And the organization can still be incoherent.

Coherence means decisions, incentives, language, trust, execution, and feedback support the same operating reality.

Transformation does not happen because people perform the process.

It happens when the system makes enough sense to move.
