## Thread Draft

1. Ambiguity is expensive.

2. It appears as delay, rework, duplicated effort, decision latency, and translation cost.

3. That is why clarity is not just communication style.

4. In complex organizations, clarity becomes operating leverage.

5. Clear priorities reduce debate about what matters.

6. Clear ownership reduces debate about who decides.

7. Clear constraints reduce debate about what is allowed.

8. The clearer the system, the less energy it wastes translating itself.
