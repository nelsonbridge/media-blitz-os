## Core Post Draft

The next DevOps question is not only how fast the pipeline can move.

It is how well the organization understands the risk of moving.

CI/CD answers whether delivery can proceed.

Governed DevOps asks:

- What changed?
- What depends on it?
- What risk does it introduce?
- What evidence supports release?
- What rollback path exists?
- What human review is required?

Agentic DevOps should not merely accelerate mechanics.

It should improve delivery judgment.
