## Thread Draft

1. Media Blitz is not the whole system. It is the publishing subsystem.

2. Medium, LinkedIn, X, Instagram, and Pinterest are distribution surfaces.

3. Publishing is downstream of knowledge construction.

4. The upstream sequence is: source material, extraction, artifact, corpus, knowledge graph.

5. Starting with platforms optimizes for output. Starting with corpus optimizes for compounding authority.

6. A public article should be an expression of structured knowledge, not a disconnected post.

7. The channel is not the architecture. The knowledge system is the architecture.

8. Build the knowledge system before scaling the publishing system.
