## Core Post Draft

The more autonomous an AI system becomes, the clearer its governance must be.

Autonomy does not eliminate accountability.

It increases the need for explicit boundaries.

What can the system do?
What must it never do?
When must it ask?
When must it stop?
Who is accountable?
Where is the evidence trail?

Human oversight cannot be a vague promise.

It has to be designed into the operating model.

Governed autonomy is the target.
