## Carousel Structure

### Slide 1

A corpus is not a folder.

### Slide 2

Source material is raw material.

Documents. Chats. Whitepapers. Notes. Diagrams.

### Slide 3

Storage preserves information.

It does not automatically create reusable knowledge.

### Slide 4

The corpus is manufactured.

Capture → Extract → Classify → Relate → Refine → Canonicalize.

### Slide 5

Every durable idea becomes an artifact.

Each artifact needs lineage, metadata, relationships, and purpose.

### Slide 6

A corpus feeds downstream systems.

Articles. Briefs. Interviews. Books. Frameworks. Strategy.

### Slide 7

The archive is not the asset.

The manufacturing process is the asset.

### Slide 8

Do not wait to find the corpus.

Build it.
