## Core Post Draft

The executive task is not merely to resolve ambiguity.

It is to convert ambiguity into capability the organization can use again.

A decision may solve the immediate issue.

Capability improves the system that will face the next issue.

That requires structure:

- name the ambiguity,
- separate knowns from assumptions,
- identify decision ownership,
- define risk boundaries,
- create feedback loops,
- preserve what the organization learns.

Ambiguity becomes valuable when it teaches the system how to move.
