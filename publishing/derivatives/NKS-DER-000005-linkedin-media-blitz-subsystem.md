## Core Post Draft

Publishing is downstream of knowledge architecture.

Medium, LinkedIn, X, Instagram, and Pinterest are distribution surfaces.

They are not the source of durable intellectual value.

That matters because many content systems start with the wrong question:

"What should we post?"

The better question is:

"What durable knowledge has already been extracted, structured, and made reusable?"

A publishing engine without an upstream knowledge system becomes improvisation.

A publishing engine fed by a corpus becomes distribution of structured value.

The channel is not the architecture.

Media Blitz creates outputs.

The broader knowledge system creates the source of those outputs.
