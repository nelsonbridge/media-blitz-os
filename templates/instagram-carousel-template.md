## Derivative Gate

An Instagram carousel inherits proof posture and claim boundaries from its source publication.

It may compress the arc visually, but it may not make stronger claims than the source artifact supports.

## Slide Structure

### Slide 1: Recognition / Title Tension

A strong, readable statement that frames a reader-recognizable problem.

### Slide 2: The Problem

What most people misunderstand or experience as friction.

### Slide 3: The Reframe

The better mental model.

### Slides 4-7: Framework

One concept per slide.

### Slide 8: Application

How to recognize or use the framework.

### Slide 9: Consequence

What changes if the old frame persists or the new frame is adopted.

### Slide 10: Invitation / CTA

Read the full Medium article, save the framework, inspect the reader's system, or continue to the next model.

## Visual Rules

- Use diagrams over stock photography.
- Maintain executive visual restraint.
- Prioritize readability.
- Avoid influencer-style clutter.
- Use consistent typography, spacing, and framework language.
- Do not exceed the proof strength of the source publication.
