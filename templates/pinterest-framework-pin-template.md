Pinterest is an evergreen discovery surface. Pins should be designed for search longevity rather than immediate engagement.

## Rules

- Make the visual useful without requiring the full article.
- Use searchable language.
- Prefer frameworks, diagrams, and decision models.
- Avoid generic quote graphics unless the quote is part of a larger framework.
