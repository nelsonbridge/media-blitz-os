## Summary

The canonical corpus of the Nelson Knowledge System does not already exist as a folder, archive, or dataset. It must be manufactured from source material through extraction, classification, refinement, relationship mapping, and canonicalization.

## Core Idea

Source material is not the corpus. The corpus is a curated output created by processing source material into durable, metadata-rich knowledge artifacts.
