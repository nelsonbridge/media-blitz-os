## Core Idea
Clarity is not merely communication quality. In executive systems, clarity behaves like an economic instrument because it reduces decision latency, coordination cost, risk premiums, and wasted motion.

## Framework
Signal → Shared Truth → Reduced Latency → Increased Trust → Faster Allocation → Better Execution → Measurable Value.

## Explanation
When organizations lack clarity, they pay hidden taxes through duplicated work, defensive reporting, delayed decisions, and political interpretation. When clarity is established around verifiable truth, the system can allocate attention, authority, and capital more efficiently.
