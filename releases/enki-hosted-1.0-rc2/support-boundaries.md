# Support Boundaries

## Enki core

Enki owns governed knowledge capture, temporal authority, provenance, policy, state transition, reconciliation, disclosure boundaries, governed retrieval, model-use controls, transaction/recovery contracts, receipts, auditability, portability, and stable consumer interfaces.

## Downstream products

Media Blitz, Career Intelligence and Placement, and Personal Cognitive Continuity consume Enki through versioned governed boundaries. They do not own or directly mutate canonical Enki state.

## Hosting providers

Cloudflare, Neon, and Google Cloud are candidate infrastructure providers only. Provider capabilities do not become Enki core semantics. The selected provider, if any, must satisfy the Enki contracts rather than redefine them.

## Production responsibility

Provider-native IAM, networking, managed database controls, key management, and secrets capabilities require provider-specific validation. Enki remains responsible for exact boundary configuration, policy, evidence binding, denial testing, rollback, and preservation of authority semantics.

Independent penetration testing must be performed by an independent authorized assessor and cannot be self-certified by Enki automation.
